# RISK ASSESSMENT

## Technical Risks

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|------------|
| DeepSeek API latency | High | Medium | Cache common patterns, fallback to Gemini |
| Gemini Arabic accuracy | High | Low | Extensive prompt testing, feedback loops |
| RTL bugs in templates | Medium | Medium | QA every sprint, automated RTL tests |
| Supabase scaling | Medium | Low | Connection pooling, regional deployment |

## Business Risks

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|------------|
| UPayments delay | High | Low | Backup: PayTabs, MyFatoorah |
| Low trial conversion | High | Medium | Multi-step paywall, A/B testing |
| High churn | High | Medium | Admin dashboard free, customer success |
| Competition entry | Medium | Medium | First-mover advantage, Arabic moat |

## Market Risks

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|------------|
| Economic downturn | Medium | Low | Low pricing, essential tool positioning |
| Regulatory changes | Low | Low | GCC compliance from day 1 |
| Currency fluctuation | Low | Medium | KWD native, local processing |

## Mitigation Summary

1. **Technical:** Extensive testing, caching, fallback systems
2. **Business:** Multiple payment providers, conversion optimization
3. **Market:** Low pricing, local compliance, unique positioning
